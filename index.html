Cookin' Good Lookin'

Your one stop destination to cooking elegance in your own home!

Today on Cookin Good Lookin, well be making seared salmon and herbed barley.

Ingredients

  • 6oz Atlantic Salmon portion (skin side cleaned)
  • High Heat cooking oil (I used avocado oil)
  • drained barley
  • handful of chopped parsely
  • Pinch of Cracked Sea Salt and Black Peppercorns
Your knife is your most trusted weapon in the kitchen. Take care of it and it will take care of you!

Instructions

1. We'll start of by cooking the barley in a pot

2. While the barley is cooking, heat up a nonstick pan onto medium high heat and pour in 1/2tbsp of oil

3. Season your Salmon with salt and pepper, then place on the pan for 2-3 min each side depending on thickness.

4. Plate the salmon with a dash of tahini and peri peri sauce (both optional) and toss in the drained barley on the pan with parsely for 1 min.

5. Get everything on the place and wow lookin good! You sure did impress me!
